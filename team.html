The People Behind the Work

One Team. All Disciplines.

Unlike firms that subcontract, we maintain a complete in-house roster of certified specialists. Every trade, one company, zero compromises.

Leadership

Nick — Principal Designer / Founder

With years of experience in residential and commercial interior design and construction, Nick founded Creations & Details on a simple belief: great spaces require a single, accountable team.

Specialist Disciplines

1. Interior Designers

Space planning, 3D concepts, and finish selection.

2. Carpenters & Joiners

Bespoke furniture, cabinetry, wardrobes and timber features.

3. Electricians

Wiring, DB board installation, lighting and smart integrations.

4. Plumbers

Water supply/drainage, bathroom and kitchen plumbing.

5. Tilers & Finishers

Floor/wall tiling, waterproofing and precision finishing.

6. Structural & Masonry

Walling, blockwork, reinforced concrete and boundary works.

7. Project Managers

Site coordination, budget/timeline oversight and reporting.

8. Landscapers

Garden planning, paving, irrigation and outdoor lighting.
