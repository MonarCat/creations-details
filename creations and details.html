Creations & Details Limited is a premier full-service design and construction firm based in Nairobi. Led by seasoned professionals, we bring together architecture, interior design, and hands-on construction expertise under one roof — eliminating the complexity of managing multiple contractors.

From concept to completion, our in-house team of specialists handles every discipline with precision. Whether you are building a dream home, renovating a commercial space, or seeking expert design consultancy, we deliver results that endure.
