Our Process

One Team. Five Steps. Zero Guesswork.

From the moment you contact us to the day we hand over your keys — here's exactly how we work.

Step 01

Consultation

We start with a site visit and honest conversation. We listen carefully to your vision, assess your space, and give you a realistic picture of what's achievable within your budget. There's no charge and no obligation at this stage.

  • Site visit at your convenience
  • Scope and feasibility assessment
  • Realistic budget guidance
  • Timeline expectations
Step 02

Design & Quotation

Our designers produce mood boards, material selections, and 3D space concepts. We then provide a detailed itemised quotation — no surprises, no hidden charges. You review, refine, and approve before a single brick is moved.

  • Mood boards and concept direction
  • 3D space planning visuals
  • Itemised, transparent quotation
  • Revision rounds until you're satisfied
Step 03

Material Sourcing

We source every material — tiles, timber, cabinetry hardware, sanitary ware, electrical fittings — to our specification and yours. Our in-house team quality-controls every delivery. No substitutions are made without your approval.

  • Supplier relationships for quality pricing
  • Quality inspection on all deliveries
  • Sample approvals before bulk orders
  • No surprise material swaps
Step 04

Build & Execute

Our craftsmen, electricians, plumbers, tilers, and finishers work to a detailed programme. Because every discipline is in-house, there's no waiting on external subcontractors — and our site supervisor keeps everything coordinated and moving.

  • In-house tradespeople across all disciplines
  • Dedicated site supervisor on every project
  • Regular progress updates with photos
  • Milestone-based payment structure
Step 05

Handover & Snag

We walk you through every finish, room by room. Any snags identified are addressed promptly within our agreed defects liability period. Your home should be exactly as you envisioned — and we don't leave until it is.

  • Full walkthrough with client
  • Snag list addressed before final payment
  • Defects liability period included
  • Post-project support available
